Kevin Blackburn-Matzen

I earned my PhD in Computer Science from Cornell University in 2016, where I was advised by Noah Snavely and Kavita Bala. My research focused on building systems that analyze large-scale photo collections to reveal patterns, trends, and changes that are difficult to perceive with the naked eye. This work spans multiple areas of computer vision, including 3D reconstruction, object recognition, and machine learning.

I grew up near a small town in Wisconsin and completed my undergraduate studies at the University of Michigan, earning dual BSE degrees in Computer Science Engineering and Computer Engineering.

From 2016 to 2021, I worked at Facebook, first on the Computational Photography team and later on the XR Tech team. I’m currently a Senior Research Scientist / Engineer at Adobe in Seattle.
