Kevin Blackburn-Matzen

I'm a Senior Research Scientist/Engineer at Adobe in Seattle, where I focus on controllable image and video generation with a particular emphasis on 3D-centric controls. My work spans computer vision, machine learning, and computational photography.

I earned my PhD in Computer Science from Cornell University in 2016 under the guidance of Noah Snavely and Kavita Bala. I received dual BSE degrees in Computer Science Engineering and Computer Engineering from the University of Michigan.

Before joining Adobe, I spent five years at Facebook (2016-2021) working on the Computational Photography and XR Tech teams. I grew up near a small town in Wisconsin.
